Accomodation - "Halkere Homes"

Haalkere Homes is a serene and peaceful home-stay located on the bank of the backwaters of Sharavathi amidst the slopes of the Western Ghats. This traditional Malnad house has a rich history of over 100 years, and it exudes an aura of tranquility that is bound to rejuvenate your senses. The Haalkere home-stay is an ideal destination for those who want to get away from the hustle and bustle of city life and experience the true beauty of Malnad. Here, you can immerse yourself in the natural splendor that surrounds you and relish the soothing calmness of the atmosphere. You will wake up to the melodious chirping of birds and the cool morning breeze that carries the refreshing fragrance of blooming flowers. The sound of water flowing through the stream that runs along the property adds to the idyllic ambience of Haalkere Homes. You can take a leisurely stroll along the stream and enjoy the greenery that thrives on its banks. The foliage of trees and shrubs fluttering in the cool breeze creates a mesmerizing vista that is sure to enchant you. At Haalkere Homes, we are committed to promoting global ecology and we take pride in being the ambassadors of nature's magic. Our goal is to provide our guests with an unforgettable experience that is both rejuvenating and inspiring. A stay at Haalkere home-stay will undoubtedly be a cherished memory that you will carry with you for a long time to come. So come and experience the magic of nature at Haalkere Homes and let us help you create lasting memories.
